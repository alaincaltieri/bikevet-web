Frequently Asked Questions

Setup & Identification

  • The identification process is robust but can make mistakes. For the best accuracy, provide a clear, well-lit profile photo of the drive-side (gears side) against a neutral background, removing excessive accessories. You can also upload additional photos.

  • Yes. After the automatic identification, you can edit both the general bike details (type, year, model) and any single component. You can also manually input the initial wear and cleanliness levels if the app could not assess them precisely.

Strava Synchronization

  • By default, the app imports only new activities. However, if you want to track wear starting from a specific past date, you can manually import your past year of Strava rides with just a few taps.

  • Yes. You can link each bike in BikeVet to its corresponding profile in your Strava equipment list. Once set up, just select the right bike in Strava when you ride, and the app will sync the data accurately.

  • By default, the app is read-only and does not ask for write permissions. However, you can opt-in to an additional feature that publishes bike milestones (e.g., reaching specific distance goals) directly to your Strava activities.

Maintenance Tracking

  • Our maintenance logic is highly advanced. We factor in weather, elevation, distance, duration, route type, and specific bike/component types. We extract this data mainly from Strava (or manual entries) and cross-reference it with our weather provider for precise wear estimation.

  • BikeVet allows you to log specific maintenance actions to recalculate component status accurately. Simply log a "replacement" action for those brake pads. You can also log visual wear assessments, specific repairs, periodic servicing (like chain lubrication), and cleanings.

Pricing & Privacy

  • The app is free for one bike and tracks six core components (e.g., chain, brake pads, tires). The premium version unlocks unlimited bikes, full tracking for every single component (from bottom brackets to freehub bearings), and comprehensive alerts. Both versions provide DIY repair guides, tool/parts recommendations, and tips to improve performance and component lifespan.

  • Account creation is not mandatory; you can use the app anonymously. Bike data is saved on our servers so it can be linked to an account later if you choose to create one. A free account allows you to sync data across devices and recover it if you lose your phone. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
